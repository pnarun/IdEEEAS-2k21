Code4Covid 2k21

Code4Covid is a national competition open to all college students.
The Hackathon's theme is "Post Covid Solutions."
You have the option of selecting one of the following Problem Domains:

  1. Healthcare
  2. Supply chain
  3. Workplace management
  4. E-learning
  5. Miscellaneous

  • Entry Fee: Rs.200/- per team.

  • 20% Discount will be provided to IEEE Membership holders.

  • All registered candidates will get the confirmation mail.

Rules:

  • Each team can register for more than one problem domain and prepare an abstract for the same (abstract format will be sent via email). The best out of all the solutions would be selected.
  • Register in a team of minimum 2 and maximum 4 participants.
  • The submissions will be reviewed and the selected teams will only be invited to participate in “Code4Covid” based on their innovative solution.
    • Date: 27th June
    • Abstract submission last date: 22nd June
    • Timing: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
