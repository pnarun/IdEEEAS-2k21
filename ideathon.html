IDEATHON

Ideathon 2021 will be a day-long event where participants will be provided with a problem statement to which they will be proposing a suitable and economically viable model for tackling the issue. The aim of the event is to innovate and gather different ideas through these models and then implement them.

  • Event starts at 10:30 AM on 26th June.

  • Entry Fee: Rs.150/- per team.(Only selected teams of round 1 will be asked to pay the registration fee.)

  • 20% Discount will be provided to IEEE Membership holders.

  • All registered candidates will get the confirmation mail.

  • Registration closes on 25th June at 12:00 AM.

  • Participation e-certificate will be provided.

  • One can participate in any number of events. There won't be a time clash between the events.

Rules:

    Round 1 - Abstract submission

  • Participants will submit an abstract and top 20 teams will be selected.
  • A deadline will be provided within which all abstracts need to be submitted.

    • Round 2 - Marketing

  • The selected teams will create a short advertisement (maximum 45 seconds) to market their product.
  • This can be done using basic video editing software or apps available.
  • Teams will be given suitable timeline for submission.
  • Based on the effectiveness of the advertisement the final 7 teams will be shortlisted.

    • Round 3 - Presentation

  • The final 7 teams will give a presentation about their idea which will include market analysis, feasibility, scalability etc.
  • The team which presents the best will be adjudged winners.

    • NOTE
  • Idea submission must be in the specfied format only.
  • The ideas from all different themes will be allowed.
  • Plagiarism of any kind will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision made by the judges and the organizing committee will be considered final.
