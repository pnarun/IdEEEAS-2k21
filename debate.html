DEBATE

A Debate is a contest where two or more speakers present their argument intent on persuading one another. It involves formal discourse on a particular topic . Logical consistency,factual accuracy and some degree of emotional appeal to the audience are elements in debating ,where one side often prevails over the other by presenting a superior "position" on the issue.
