About IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest professional association advancing innovation and technological excellence for the benefit of humanity. Through its highly-cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities, it has become the trusted voice for engineering, computing and technology information around the globe. We, IEEE SIT SB, come under Bangalore section of Region 10.

IEEE SIT Student Branch was established in Siddaganga Institute of Technology in the year 2002, under the leadership of Prof. Basavarajaiah, Head of the Department, Electronics and Communication. Currently, it is being mentored by Dr. K Vishwanath. WIE IEEE SIT SB is an affinity group which was established in 2009, currently being mentored by Dr. Veena Karjigi. IEEE SIT SB is the only Student Branch from Banglore Section to get published in IEEE India Council Newsletter, 2019, also winning Outstanding Small Student Branch award under the Bangalore Section in the same year.

Being called the largest technical group in the college, we conduct various coding competition, student activities, events, and workshops. IdEEEas is the flagship event of IEEE SIT SB organaised every year. It is a national-level technical symposium, that provides one of the biggest platforms to test knowledge, skills and compete with students from all over the nation.
