PHOTOGRAPHIA

“Good dreaming is what leads to good photographs.” So here comes the exciting and interesting event you have ever come across. Photographia is an artistic program organized by the team IEEE SIT SB in the event IdEEEas. It is about finding something interesting in an ordinary place. All you need is a mobile phone and some creativity. We promise, you will have fun while participating in this event.

Theme:

It will be given on the event day.

  • Event starts at 10:00 AM on 27th June.

  • Entry Fee: Rs.50/- per participant.

  • 20% Discount will be provided to IEEE Membership holders.

  • All registered candidates will get the confirmation mail.

  • Registration closes on 26th June at 12:00 AM.

  • Participation e-certificate will be provided.

  • One can participate in any number of events. There won't be a time clash between events.

Rules:

  • Only photos taken from a mobile phone will be accepted.
  • Only soft copies will be accepted.
  • Participants will have to take the photos on the same day within allotted time.
  • Photographs should be unedited.
  • Coordinator decision will be final & binding.

    Selection Criteria:

    First Round:

  • Registered participants will get a mail on the day of event in which they need to fill the google form within a specified time. An on spot theme will be given. Participant need to click a picture using cell phone out of the theme and upload the same within the given time.

    • Second Round:

  • Top ten participants will be selected from the first round. They will be noticed through mail. Then a meeting will be scheduled over which the top 10 contestants need to explain their view on the content in their picture. The best explanation with the best photo will be rewarded
