RECTIFIER

One of the major skills of an engineering student should be finding and resolving defects or problems within a system which hinders its functioning, and the same is tested in the participants through this contest. The participants will be provided with a circuit with minor faults, which they have to debug and get the appropriate output. Analysing electronic circuits and applying theoretical knowledge into a practical scenario is the main focus of this event.

  • Event starts at 11:30 AM on 27th June.

  • Entry Fee: Rs.50/- per participant.

  • 20% Discount will be provided to IEEE Membership holders.

  • All registered candidates will get the confirmation mail.

  • Registration closes on 26th June at 12:00 AM.

  • Participation e-certificate will be provided.

  • One can participate in any number of events. There won't be a time clash between events.

Rules:

  • In the preliminary round, participants are given within a test that has to be solved in 30 minutes.
  • The questions comprise Multiple choice questions (MCQs) and Reasoning
  • The top five participants will be selected for finals.
  • The final round involves debugging of the given circuit.
  • Simulators:
    • Tinkercad
    • Ltspice
    • NI Simulator
  • Participants are requested to arrange PCs.
  • Abide by the rules made by the organiser.
  • Malpractice is not entertained.
  • Decisions of the judges and coordinators are final.
