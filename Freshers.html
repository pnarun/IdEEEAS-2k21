inIEEEzio

InIEEEzio , an event exclusively for freshers, is filled with a whole lot of fun. As a fresher, it's your time to enjoy, have fun and learn many a things as well ! So , if you want to build your knowledge , rejuvenate and revitalize yourself , then you mustn't be missing InIEEEzio!

  • Registration is FREE for this Event and it is exclusively for First year students of SIT, Tumkur.

  • Event Round 1 and Round 2 will be conducted on 26th June, starts at 01:15 PM.

  • Event Round 3 will be conducted on 27th June, starts at 04:00 PM.

  • Registration closes on 25th June at 12:00 AM.

  • One can participate in any number of events. There won't be a time clash between events.

Rules:

  • The event is exclusively for freshers . Only first year students are allowed to participate.
  • Participants are informed to have stable internet conection, the event will be completely proctored.
  • Participants will compete as indivduals
  • The event will have three rounds: a priliminary round , a second round followed by the final round.
  • In priliminary round the participants will be given a techno fun quiz.
  • Second round will have brain teasers.
  • Third round will be a surprise round.
  • Platform used : Kahoot.
    Participants need to go to https://kahoot.it/ or download Kahoot application from playstore.
    Enter the game pin (which will be informed prior to the start of the game).
    Enter name (name_usn format).
    Click on start to play the game !!.
